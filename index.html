About me

I am currently a fifth-year Phd student in School of Mathematical Sciences at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Ligang Liu. Before that, I recevied my B.S. in Wendian College from Anhui University in 2015.

I was a visiting student at the Visual Computing Center, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) from October 2016 to June 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Peter Wonka .

My research interests are in the area of computer graphics and computer vision.
