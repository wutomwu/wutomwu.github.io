Bio

I obtained my B.S from Anhui University (AHU) in 2015 and my Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2020. My Ph.D. supervisor was Prof. Ligang Liu and Prof. Xiao-Ming Fu. I worked in Visual Computing Center at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) as a visiting student under the supervision of Prof. Peter Wonka during 2016-2017. I joined School of Computer Science and Information Engineering in Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) since Jul. 2020. My research interests include computer graphics and computer vision. I received the Outstanding Young Scientist Award (优秀青年科学家奖) from Anhui Computer Society in 2023.
