Bio

I obtained my B.S from Anhui University (AHU) in 2015 and my Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2020. My Ph.D. supervisor was Dr. Ligang Liu and Dr. Xiao-Ming Fu. I worked in Visual Computing Center at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) as a visiting student under the supervision of Dr. Peter Wonka during 2016-2017. I joined School of Computer Science and Information Engineering in Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) since Jul. 2020. My research interests include computer graphics and computer vision.
